Phuket Patong day 1

7 December 2024 · 1 min read

Today started around 2am and violent food poisoning. I cannot express how much liquid was expelled from my body and with such force. It was relentless and carried on for most of the morning, and the runs ongoing. Bedridden, hallucinating, toilet hugging, stomach cramping, sweating, shaking…..I thought the end was nigh. Mel supported throughout with probiotics, electrolytes, etc. Mel went for lunch and had a club sandwich and fries and even ventured the 2 minute walk to the 711 before retreating back to the safety of the womb (room). What a waste of a day for us. I never left the room / bathroom. But we did watch Jaws 2, so that’s something.
