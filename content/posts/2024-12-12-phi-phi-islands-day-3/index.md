Mel fancied a lie in, and as it was slightly overcast, I decided to go for a hike to see all 3 viewpoints on the island, so grabbed a bottle of water and off I went. Wow, was it humid...I was absolutely drenched 10 minutes in and about 3km to go. Uphill.

![](phiphid3%201%20(2).jpg)

Took about an hour to get to checkpoint 3 via road and jungle. Stunning views and a 20 Baht entrance fee.

![](phiphid3%201%20(4).jpg)

![](phiphid3%201%20(5).jpg)

![](phiphid3%201%20(6).jpg)

Then the short 0.5k drop to checkpoint 2 which was lovely too.

![](phiphid3%201%20(7).jpg)

![](phiphid3%201%20(8).jpg)

Then another 2.5km walk back down and then 350 steps up to checkpoint 1. I was dying by this point but couldn't let it beat me. 50 baht entrance to this one (cynically milk the tourist as this is the easiest in isolation).
