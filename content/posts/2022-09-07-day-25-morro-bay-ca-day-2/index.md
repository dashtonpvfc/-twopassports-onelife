date: 2022-09-07
destination: "USA 2022"

Up at 10am for our free Cinnamon Buns as part of the hotel complimentary breakfast, Mel queued for a good 45 minutes....in the wrong bakery! Hahha, only needed to go the opposite side and pick them up, oh how i laughed!!! Walked to the rock of Morro and onto the beach for a spot of sun bathing and listening to podcast (Joe Rogan and Mark Zuckerberg) took binoculars to study the sea otters and sea lions, so cute!! Walked back, had lunch at The Dockside, I had Clam Chowder in a bread bowl, so so good! Mel had fish and chips, not so good...and a couple of diet cokes each. I then went kayaking, mel chickened out and went shopping. What a great experience! Kayaked to where the sea lions were hanging out, absolutely brilliant seeing them interact with each other, like a group of angry p\*issed up old men arguing and grunting at each other non stop! Mel greeted me as i got off and realised i was very very burnt. Slow walk back to room, then out at 6:30 ish, town very quiet now Labor day weekend has finished, as in everywhere we have been in America, everywhere shuts down at 8:30pm to 9pm....how very odd. Went to brewery for one, then struggled to get in a restaurant but managed one called  Rose's bar and grill ( I wish it was Rozey's - gagging for a Balti ).....I had Linguini and Clam pasta, Mel had Crab sandwich, i asked for a bottle of wine, he brought a glass, not happy, Mel also had a couple of 12 dollar cocktails and a Creme Brulee.....overall food pretty cac for $120....paid through gritted teeth and walked home sulking.
