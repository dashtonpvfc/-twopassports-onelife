Had a lie in and completely missed breakfast, lazy trouts. Left Mel snoozing and went for a walk to explore...Ao Nang is lovely but the weather has been frankly, disappointing since Chiang Mai, no blue skies or sun....just overcast with the odd shower mixed in.

![](krabi%20day%202%201%20(1).jpg)

![](krabi%20day%202%201%20(2).jpg)

Walked a couple of miles to the extreme east of the coastline of Ao Nang and sampled a Chang with a view. Then wandered up the road to a random restaurant as there was a pizza offer on...169 Baht for any pizza on the menu... naturally I had the seafood one as it represented the most bang for your buck. This was washed down with a Chang. All in about 7 quid so mustn't grumble.

![](krabi%20day%202%201%20(3).jpg)

![](krabi%20day%202%201%20(4).jpg)

I then frequented another rooftop bar to people watch and listen to podcasts...just finished up Joe Rogan with Quentin Tarantino, an excellent listen. Had a stroll on the beach and then then headed north up the main Ao Nang stretch.

![](krabi%20day%202%201%20(6).jpg)

![](krabi%20day%202%201%20(7).jpg)

Found a fantastic little cocktail bar with happy hour (that lasted all day) with cocktails at 100 baht each - about £2.39. Had a couple of Cuba Libres whilst listening to music and watching the world go by. I then moved on to another cafe with a similar deal, so naturally, I tested it out.

![](krabi%20day%202%201%20(8).jpg)

![](krabi%20day%202%201%20(9).jpg)

![](krabi%20day%202%201%20(10).jpg)

Had another cocktail at a pop up bar on the street and Met sleepy Mel at the same curry house as last night! Tandoori Knight -  Mel had Dopiaza and I had the same as last night. Both were gorgeous! Headed home, bed at 10:30pm.
