Woke and had lovely breakfast at hotel around 9ish - on my own as Mel needed a lie in. Then woke her up and went for a final walk around this lovely town, was about 12 midday by now and sleepy was ravenous.

![](aonangd4%20(1).jpg)

![](aonangd4%20(2).jpg)

Straight to the curry house she said, chicken tikka massala, rice and garlic naan was devoured in minutes...I had a diet coke. Put a towel down on the beach and went for a swim, weather still not great but the sea is like a warm bath.

![](aonangd4%20(3).jpg)

![](aonangd4%20(4).jpg)

We then retreated for a coffee and went back to the room to get ready for the chilled evening. We found a craft ale house and had a Vedett IPA and Pilsner respectively and then headed for a low key Thai restaurant that had very good reviews.

![](aonangd4%20(5).jpg)

![](aonangd4%20(6).jpg)

We shared a pork Gyoza starter and then I had Chicken Massaman curry which was nice but far too sweet and Mel had a chicken stir fry with noodles and a fried egg on top. Headed to bed around 19:30 feeling slightly exhausted.
