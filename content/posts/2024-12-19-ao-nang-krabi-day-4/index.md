---
title: "Ao Nang Krabi day 4"
date: 2024-2-19
destination: "Thailand 2024"
---

Woke and had lovely breakfast at hotel around 9ish - on my own as Mel needed a lie in. Then woke her up and went for a final walk around this lovely town, was about 12 midday by now and sleepy was ravenous. Straight to the curry house she said, chicken tikka massala, rice and garlic naan was devoured in minutes...I had a diet coke. Put a towel down on the beach and went for a swim, weather still not great but the sea is like a warm bath. We then retreated for a coffee and went back to the room to get ready for the chilled evening. We found a craft ale house and had a Vedett IPA and Pilsner respectively and then headed for a low key Thai restaurant that had very good reviews. We shared a pork Gyoza starter and then I had Chicken Massaman curry which was nice but far too sweet and Mel had a chicken stir fry with noodles and a fried egg on top. Headed to bed around 19:30 feeling slightly exhausted.
