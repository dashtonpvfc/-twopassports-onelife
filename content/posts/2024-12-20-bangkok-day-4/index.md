6am start for the 8:30am flight to Bangkok from Krabi, flight on time for first time this holiday - weather again glorious! Arrived about 10am at hotel so dumped bags and went for a Thai tea for me and juice for Mel at a roadside joint.

![](bangkokd4%201%20(1).jpg)

![](bangkokd4%201%20(2).jpg)

Went to use pool at about 11am - what a gorgeous pool! Planes taking off right next to us, which was interesting to us plane geeks and Mel tried out the gym.

![](bangkokd4%201%20(3).jpg)

![](bangkokd4%201%20(4).jpg)

Went for a roadside lunch about 12:45 - I had spicy beef and rice (wasn't spicy) and Mel a chicken fried rice concoction. Was nice if a bit on the stingy side but at about £3 all in with water we can't complain.

![](bangkokd4%201%20(5).jpg)

![](bangkokd4%201%20(6).jpg)

Headed to 711 next to the hotel and picked up a 4 pack of Singha to consume during the afternoon pool session. Mel had a bottle of water.

![](bangkokd4%201%20(7).jpg)

![](bangkokd4%201%20(8).jpg)

Spent a lovely afternoon sunbathing, swimming, drinking and plane spotting.

![](bangkokd4%201%20(9).jpg)

![](bangkokd4%201%20(10).jpg)

![](bangkokd4%201%20(11).jpg)

Got dressed and went out early doors as another early start tomorrow. Headed for a local restaurant for a beer - found Paulaner Dunkel on the menu but it was served actually semi frozen! And so was the glass! Mel's also - how the hell can you get it wrong?

![](bangkokd4%201%20(12).jpg)

![](bangkokd4%201%20(13).jpg)

![](bangkokd4%201%20(14).jpg)
