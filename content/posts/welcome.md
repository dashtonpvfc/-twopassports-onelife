Hello, and welcome to our little corner of the internet.

We're Danny and Mel — a couple from the UK who've spent the better part of our lives dreaming about travel, and the last few years actually doing it. This blog is our way of documenting the trips, the mishaps, the brilliant meals, the wrong turns, and all the moments in between.

We're not travel experts, influencers, or people with any particular claim to authority on the matter. We're just two people who got bitten by the travel bug and can't quite shake it.

## Why "Two Passports, One Life"?

Because that's really what it comes down to. Two people, two little blue books, and one shared life spent trying to see as much of this extraordinary world as possible.

We'll be posting about everywhere we go — from weekend breaks in Europe to long-haul adventures further afield. Expect honest accounts, lots of photos, and the occasional overly enthusiastic write-up of a meal we can't stop thinking about.

Thanks for being here. We hope you enjoy following along as much as we enjoy going.

*Danny & Mel*
